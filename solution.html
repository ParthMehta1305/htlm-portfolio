Common Questions

What is Grey Market Premium (GMP)?
The Grey Market Premium (GMP) is the unofficial price at which an IPO trades in the grey market before listing. It indicates potential listing performance; a positive GMP suggests a profitable debut.
How can I increase my allotment chances?
To increase your chances, apply from multiple Demat accounts of family members, bid at the upper price band, and ensure sufficient funds in your bank account.
Can I cancel my IPO application?
Retail investors can cancel applications while the IPO is open. NII and QIB applications typically cannot be cancelled once submitted.
